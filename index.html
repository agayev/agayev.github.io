I am a PhD student in the Computer Science Department at Carnegie Mellon University advised by George Amvrosiadis and working closely with Greg Ganger. In the past, I have worked with Garth Gibson and Eric Xing. I have broad research interests in computer systems, including storage and file systems, distributed systems, and operating systems.

I will join Penn State EECS as an assistant professor in Fall 2020.
